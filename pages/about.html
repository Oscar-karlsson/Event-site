About

Our Story

EntertainEd is not just an event management company; it's a journey of passion and creativity that has been bringing people together for over three decades. Founded by the visionary CEO, Sarah Johnson, EntertainEd has evolved into a powerhouse of entertainment, orchestrating unforgettable moments, creating joy, and redefining what it means to have a good time.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple yet profound: to craft and curate the most vibrant, exciting, and entertaining events that will leave you exhilarated and wanting more. We believe that life is a series of moments, and we're here to make every moment a masterpiece.

What We Do

At EntertainEd, we specialize in designing and hosting a diverse range of events that cater to every taste and age group. From music festivals that transcend genres to comedy nights that tickle your funny bone, from immersive theater experiences to culinary adventures that tantalize your taste buds, we offer an array of entertainment options that promise something for everyone.
