Our Story

For over three decades, EntertainEd has been a beacon of creativity, passion, and innovation in the world of entertainment. Founded by Sarah Johnson, a visionary with an unshakable love for creating unforgettable moments, EntertainEd's journey has been nothing short of extraordinary. It all began with a dream, a dream to redefine what it means to have a good time, to create lasting memories, and to make every moment a masterpiece. Sarah's journey in the event management industry spans more than 30 years, during which she transformed EntertainEd from a humble start-up into a powerhouse of creativity and innovation. With her dedication, vision, and an innate ability to turn dreams into reality, Sarah has led the company through countless successful events, leaving attendees exhilarated and wanting more. But the story of EntertainEd is not just about Sarah; it's about a team of passionate individuals who share the same dedication to making every event extraordinary. It's about our loyal customers who have been with us on this incredible journey, supporting us as we continue to create joy and redefine entertainment.

What We Do

At EntertainEd, we specialize in designing and hosting a diverse range of events that cater to every taste and age group. We're dedicated to making every moment memorable, and we do that by offering a variety of entertainment options. Here's a glimpse of what we do:

Music & Entertainment: We bring together music enthusiasts of all genres to enjoy live performances, music festivals, and unforgettable music experiences.

Comedy & Entertainment: Laughter is the best medicine, and our comedy nights provide the perfect dose of hilarity, offering a fantastic afterwork experience for friends and colleagues.

Culinary Adventures: Foodies can embark on a global culinary adventure with our food truck festivals and gourmet experiences, tantalizing taste buds with international flavors.

Arts & Entertainment: We celebrate the world of arts through creative craft fairs, art exhibitions, and interactive workshops that inspire creativity.

Unique Experiences: Our immersive and unique events take attendees on thrilling journeys, from solving mysteries in mansions to exploring the latest in technology and innovation.

Community & Charity: At EntertainEd, we believe in giving back. We host events that support our local communities and charitable causes, making a positive impact.

Our events are carefully curated to provide a little something for everyone, ensuring that every moment spent with EntertainEd is a showstopper and an adventure.
